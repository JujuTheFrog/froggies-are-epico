Frogs are great!

Frogs have been kept as pet's since the 1970's. Most people like to keep poison darts. Colorful little buddies.Personally, the dumber the better. I got one white tree frog and a toad we found on the road. He had broken legs so we took him in until he could walk again. Sadly, he never fully recovered so he'll be our pet until the end!

My life goal is to give my future reptiles the best life possible!

Most people think toads are poisonous, but unless you're a cat or dog and thinking a jumpy slimy creature is looking tasty, then you'll be fine!
